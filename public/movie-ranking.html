The Best Movies According to Rohit

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A story about hope and friendship set inside a prison. Andy Dufresne, wrongly convicted, never gives up on freedom.

The Dark Knight (2008)

Batman faces his greatest test when the Joker unleashes chaos in Gotham.

Inception (2010)

A mind-bending sci-fi thriller where thieves steal secrets by entering dreams.
